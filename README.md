# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales Analysis

## Project Overview
This project presents an in-depth analysis of an e-commerce dataset (Sample Superstore) to uncover key business insights related to sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and product trends. The goal is to support data-driven decision-making using exploratory data analysis (EDA).

---

## Objectives
- Analyze monthly sales and identify peak & low-performing periods  
- Evaluate sales and profit across categories and sub-categories  
- Identify high and low profitability areas  
- Understand customer segment contribution  
- Examine sales-to-profit efficiency  

---

## Dataset
- **Name:** Sample Superstore  
- **Records:** 9994  
- **Features:**  
  - Order Date, Sales, Profit  
  - Category, Sub-Category  
  - Customer Segment  
  - Region, Ship Mode  

---

## Tools & Technologies
- **Python** (Pandas, NumPy)  
- **Data Visualization:** Matplotlib, Seaborn  
- **Environment:** Jupyter Notebook  

---

## Key Analysis Performed

### Monthly Sales & Profit Analysis
- Identified seasonal trends and revenue peaks  
- Highest sales observed in November  
- Profit peaks in December  

### Category & Sub-Category Analysis
- Technology is the top-performing category  
- Furniture shows weak profitability  
- Tables identified as a loss-making sub-category  

### Customer Segment Analysis
- Consumer segment contributes the highest sales and profit  
- Home Office segment shows better efficiency (low sales-to-profit ratio)  

### Sales-to-Profit Ratio
- Used to evaluate business efficiency  
- Lower ratio indicates better profitability  

---

## Key Insights
- Sales are highly seasonal, peaking in Q4  
- High revenue does not always mean high profit  
- Discount-heavy categories reduce profitability  
- Product-level analysis is critical for decision-making  

---

## Recommendations
- Focus on high-margin categories like Technology  
- Reduce discounts on loss-making products (e.g., Tables)  
- Optimize pricing and inventory for better profitability  
- Target high-efficiency customer segments
